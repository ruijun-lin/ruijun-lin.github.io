Ruijun Lin 林睿军

PhD student in Mathematics at the University of Wollongong

I am a PhD student in Mathematics at the University of Wollongong, working under the supervision of Professor Aidan Sims. My research lies in the area of operator algebras, with a particular focus on k-graphs (higher-rank graphs) and their associated groupoids and C*-algebras.

Before my PhD, I completed my master studies at the University of Copenhagen and my undergraduate studies at Southern University of Science and Technology.
