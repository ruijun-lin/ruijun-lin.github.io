Interests & Hobbies

Beyond mathematics, here are some things I enjoy:

Music

Music has always been a part of my life. I learned to play the erhu (a traditional Chinese instrument) as a child, and after graduating from high school, I began learning the piano. I love both instruments deeply. My favorite genres include classical music, new age, and traditional Chinese music.

The following is one my piano covers In the Morning Light composed by Yanni. It is the first piece of music totally practiced by myself.

Railway Infrastructures

I am interested in the railway transportation stuff in China and the world, especially the high-speed rail and metro lines that have been built, under construction and under planning.
